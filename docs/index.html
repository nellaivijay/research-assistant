🎯 Why Enhanced Research Assistant?

Modern research requires managing overwhelming amounts of literature while extracting meaningful insights. The Enhanced Research Assistant combines AI-powered analysis with practical workflow management to help researchers:

  • Discover Relevant Papers - Multi-source recommendations with intelligent ranking
  • Analyze Efficiently - AI-powered summarization and insight extraction
  • Manage Workflow - Reading lists, notes, and progress tracking
  • Optimize Costs - Smart model selection and A/B testing
  • Collaborate Easily - Export and share in standard formats

🚀 Quick Start

  1. Visit the live application
  2. Enter a paper ID or URL to get recommendations
  3. Add interesting papers to your reading list
  4. Configure AI models for analysis (optional)
  5. Use A/B testing to find your preferred models
  6. Export your reading list in your preferred format
